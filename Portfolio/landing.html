EXP Realty

Virtual Assistant – April 2022 – March 2023

Embarking on my journey as a Virtual Assistant at EXP Realty from April 2022 to March 2023 was like stepping into a whirlwind of opportunities, and I had the pleasure of working alongside Hera Boncaros. In those 11 months, I found myself wearing many hats, each one adding a new layer to my adventure in the world of real estate and administration.

During my 11 months as a Virtual Assistant at EXP Realty, I navigated a whirlwind of opportunities alongside Hera Boncaros. From managing a massive contact tracing database with over a thousand entries, where attention to detail became second nature, to taking charge of EXP Realty's social media, turning regular updates into creative buzz for potential clients.

Crafting and editing content for the company's website was a delightful balancing act of creativity and tech-savviness. The Facebook Marketplace was my digital playground, ensuring every interaction felt like a virtual handshake in the vibrant world of real estate.

But it didn't end there. As a backstage crew member, I supported various projects within the Real Estate Company, from marketing initiatives to client communications. Each day brought a new adventure, and I loved every moment of contributing to the team's success.

Looking back, my time as a Virtual Assistant wasn't just about tasks on a to-do list; it was a journey of growth, learning, and getting my hands dirty in the real-world dynamics of the real estate game. EXP Realty wasn't just a workplace; it was a community where each day brought a new challenge and a chance to make a real impact.
